the structure of the lang files are:
first level in Capital Letters provides the Kategorie:
- Types
- Sheets
- Prompts
- etc

the second level with a capital first letter provides Area in the Kategorie
- Actor
- Items
or 
- Attributes
- Characteristics

The third and further level with all small letters provides the actual field/name
- dexterity
- physicalDefense
- etc.
